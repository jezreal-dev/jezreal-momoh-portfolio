# Visual Identity & Creative Guidelines: Forge Theme

This guide establishes the visual identity for the Jezreal Momoh brand, moving away from generic AI/developer cyan palettes to a custom, warm tech aesthetic.

---

## 1. The "Forge" Color Palette
This palette balances technical precision with warmth and founder ambition:

*   **Primary Background (`#0D0D0D`)**: Warm, near-black. Feels more organic and human than clinical dark gray.
*   **Primary Accent (`#E8630A`)**: Burnt orange/ember. Signals drive, ambition, and fire. Differentiates your brand from the standard blue/cyan tech crowd.
*   **Secondary Text/Foreground (`#F5F0E8`)**: Warm off-white/cream. Highly readable and softer on the eyes than pure white.
*   **Secondary Background/Cards (`#1C2B3A`)**: Deep navy-slate. Anchors technical credibility and trust.
*   **Highlight Accent (`#FFBE0B`)**: Amber. Used sparingly for key actions, warnings, and emphasis.

---

## 2. Visual Style & Assets
*   **Themes**: High-signal, functional, clean, and real. Avoid standard corporate stock photos or generic "AI neon hacker" styles.
*   **Asset Styles**:
    *   *Terminal Overlays*: Code blocks, terminal sessions (using `#0D0D0D` background and `#E8630A` text/highlights).
    *   *System Architecture Diagrams*: Clean, isometric 3D charts displaying data flow, databases, or API integrations.
    *   *Developer Hardware*: Aesthetic, low-light close-ups of mechanical keyboards, microchips, or terminal setups.

---

## 3. Image Prompt Guidelines (For Nano Banana)
When generating graphics for blog covers, LinkedIn banners, or guides:
*   **Style**: Industrial tech, warm minimalist, sharp lines.
*   **Lighting**: High-contrast, warm ember glow, soft shadows, dark-mode terminal light.
*   **Key Prompt Terms**: "isometric systems architecture diagram, warm dark gray background, glowing orange and amber paths, technical drawing, high-fidelity".
*   **Avoid**: High-contrast blue/cyan neon, generic human stock photos, messy wireframes, or low-contrast text overlays.
