# Skill: Marketing Intelligence & Competitor Strategy Extraction

This skill defines the execution logic for the **Research Analyst** role. It instructs the agent on how to query NotebookLM sources, extract strategic insights from competitor research, and map them back to our brand's core strategy.

## Goal
To produce a detailed Competitor Strategy Briefing that is:
1.  **Grounded**: Fully backed by the sources inside our NotebookLM workspace.
2.  **Structured**: Outlined according to our marketing intelligence playbook.
3.  **Actionable**: Directly applicable to refining Sunday Bake's positioning.

## Execution Instructions

When running competitor analysis, execute the following four phases:

### Phase 1: Extract Positioning Mechanics
*   Identify the competitor's core value propositions.
*   Determine their pricing tiers, product categorization, and messaging themes.
*   Detail where they position themselves in the market (e.g., luxury, convenience, organic).

### Phase 2: Extract Emotional Triggers
*   Analyze customer reviews and media coverage to find emotional hooks.
*   Identify why customers buy from them (e.g., status, nostalgia, comfort).
*   Document any recurring friction points or negative customer feedback.

### Phase 3: Extract Strategic Patterns
*   Analyze their marketing distribution channels (e.g., social, email, physical locations).
*   Identify their content themes and creative hooks.

### Phase 4: Synthesize & Adapt
*   Synthesize the findings into a SWOT-like strategic matrix.
*   Outline 3 key takeaways that Sunday Bake can adapt to improve its own positioning.
*   Save the output in `outputs/` under a folder named after the target competitor.
